Core Services

Comprehensive solutions designed for speed, trust, and scalability

01

Vehicle & Parts Trading

Access a wide inventory of vehicles and spare parts sourced from trusted global suppliers and auctions. Buyers can place orders, view inspection reports, and manage orders through one channel.

02

Auction Access & Bidding Engine

Participate in live vehicle auctions across regions. Our system provides real-time bidding, multi-market listings, and predictive pricing insights to maximize deal value.

03

Logistics & Customs Management

We manage end-to-end cross-border logistics, from vehicle pickup to delivery including customs clearance, shipping, inspection coordination, and real-time tracking.

04

Compliance & Risk Management

Our operations are fully compliance-led. We handle AML/KYC verification, trade documentation, ESG monitoring, and ensure that every transaction adheres to regulatory standards.

05

Strategic Local Partner (SLP) Model

We operate locally through vetted Strategic Local Partners who manage onboarding, dealer relationships, compliance, logistics, and support, ensuring cultural alignment and trust.

06

Escrow & Secure Payment Solutions

All transactions are safeguarded through Globe XM's escrow engine, protecting both buyers and sellers by ensuring milestone-based fund release.

07

Dealer Financing Enablement

Dealers can access credit through Globe XM's financing framework, including partnerships with financial providers and internal credit line options to scale purchasing capacity.

08

Data & Market Intelligence

Our platform provides advanced analytics and insights, pricing trends, inventory demand, dealer performance to help dealers make smarter, faster decisions.
